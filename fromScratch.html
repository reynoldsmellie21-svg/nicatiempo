About My Trip

I traveled to Nicaragua to help teach English to children. This experience helped me grow in communication, patience, and cultural understanding!

What We Did Each Day

  1. Morning routine
    • Walk to the local gym
    • explore Granada
    • Lesson Prep
  2. Afternoon: Teaching!
    • Eat lunch prepared by our cook Lorena
    • Walk to school
    • Teach younger block of kids ages 4-5
    • Teacher older block ages 6-17
  3. Evening schenanigans
    • Eat premade dinner or go out to eat!
    • Stop by the store
    • Choose your own adventure: Swimming, exploring, playing games
